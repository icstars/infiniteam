What does spending money mean?

Spending is the opposite of saving. It means handing over your your money in exchange for goods. (book or crayons for example)

What the Spend Slot is for?

It's where you put the money for the less expensive things you want to buy now, like snacks, little toys or magazine.

    Smart spending tips:
  • Make a list! Write down all the little things you'd like to buy with your Spend money. This will help you remember what your goal are.
  • Then choose the item you need to buy the most.
  • Who's #1? You making spending choices every time you buy something. You can't buy everything on your list, so you need to decide which choice you need the most.
  • Compare prices! Smart spenders find the lowest price for whatever they want to buy.
