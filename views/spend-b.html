What does spending money mean?

Spending means handing over your your money in exchange for goods.

What the Spend Slot is for?

It's where you put the money for the less expensive things you want to buy now, like little snacks or toys.

    Smart spending tips:
  • Make a list!
  • Then choose the item you need to buy the most.
  • Set your spending goals.
  • Try to find the lowest prices.
