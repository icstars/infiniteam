What does saving money means?

Saving means not spending your money now, but putting it away for spending it in the future.

Why I should save money?

Because the more money you save, the more you can buy later.

You could either spend $1 now on an inexpensive toy that will probably break, or you could save many $1 bills and be able to buy that new book or pensils you want.
